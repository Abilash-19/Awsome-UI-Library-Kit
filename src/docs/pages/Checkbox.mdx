Checkboxes allow users to select one or more items from a set.

## Basic Usage
<ComponentPreview code={`<Checkbox label="Accept terms and conditions" />`}>
  <Checkbox label="Accept terms and conditions" />
</ComponentPreview>

## States
Checkboxes can have descriptions, show errors, or be indeterminate.

<ComponentPreview code={`<Checkbox 
  label="Marketing emails" 
  description="Receive updates about new products and features." 
  defaultChecked 
/>`}>
  <Checkbox 
    label="Marketing emails" 
    description="Receive updates about new products and features." 
    defaultChecked 
  />
</ComponentPreview>

### Indeterminate
<ComponentPreview code={`<Checkbox label="Select all" indeterminate />`}>
  <Checkbox label="Select all" indeterminate />
</ComponentPreview>
