Inputs are fundamental form components for collecting user text, numbers, or passwords. They provide built-in validation feedback, icon support, and theme-aware styling.

## Basic Usage
A standard input includes a label, a placeholder, and optional helper text.

<ComponentPreview code={`<Input 
  label="Email Address" 
  placeholder="Enter your email" 
  helperText="We'll never share your data."
/>`}>
  <div className="w-full max-w-sm mx-auto">
    <Input label="Email Address" placeholder="Enter your email" helperText="We'll never share your data." />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>

## Customization
### Icons & Loading
Enhance meaning with leading or trailing icons, or show a loading indicator.

<ComponentPreview code={`<Input label="Search" leftIcon={<span>🔍</span>} placeholder="Search..." />
<Input label="Password" rightIcon={<span>👁️</span>} type="password" />
<Input isLoading label="Validating..." placeholder="Checking availability..." />`}>
  <div className="w-full max-w-sm mx-auto space-y-6">
    <Input label="Search Library" leftIcon={<span>🔍</span>} placeholder="Search components..." />
    <Input label="Secure Password" rightIcon={<span className="opacity-40">👁️</span>} type="password" placeholder="••••••••" />
    <Input isLoading label="Checking Username" placeholder="Validating..." defaultValue="abilash_19" />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>

### Validation States
Clear visual cues help users correct errors or confirm success.

<ComponentPreview code={`<Input label="Username" error="This username is already taken" defaultValue="john_doe" />`}>
  <div className="w-full max-w-sm mx-auto">
    <Input 
      label="Username" 
      error="This handle is currently unavailable." 
      defaultValue="admin"
    />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>

### Sizes
Inputs support three sizes: `sm`, `md` (default), and `lg`.

<ComponentPreview code={`<Input inputSize="sm" ... />
<Input inputSize="md" ... />
<Input inputSize="lg" ... />`}>
  <div className="w-full max-w-sm mx-auto space-y-4">
    <Input inputSize="sm" placeholder="Small (32px high)" />
    <Input inputSize="md" placeholder="Medium (40px high)" />
    <Input inputSize="lg" placeholder="Large (48px high)" />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>
