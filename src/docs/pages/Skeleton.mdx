## Basic Usage

Combine shapes to mock a content card during loading.

<ComponentPreview code={`<Skeleton variant="circular" width={64} height={64} />
<Skeleton variant="text" width="80%" />
<Skeleton variant="rectangular" height={100} />`}>
  <div className="w-full max-w-sm p-5 border rounded-xl space-y-4" style={{ borderColor: 'var(--color-border)' }}>
    <div className="flex items-center gap-3">
      <Skeleton variant="circular" width={48} height={48} />
      <div className="space-y-2 flex-1">
        <Skeleton variant="text" width="60%" height={14} />
        <Skeleton variant="text" width="40%" height={10} />
      </div>
    </div>
    <Skeleton variant="rectangular" height={120} className="rounded-lg" />
    <div className="space-y-2">
      <Skeleton variant="text" height={10} />
      <Skeleton variant="text" height={10} />
      <Skeleton variant="text" width="70%" height={10} />
    </div>
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>

## Animations

Wave sweeps across the surface. Pulse fades in and out. None is static.

<ComponentPreview code={`<Skeleton animation="wave" />
<Skeleton animation="pulse" />
<Skeleton animation="none" />`}>
  <div className="space-y-3 w-full">
    <div className="flex flex-col gap-1">
      <span className="text-[10px] font-semibold uppercase opacity-30">Wave</span>
      <Skeleton animation="wave" height={36} />
    </div>
    <div className="flex flex-col gap-1">
      <span className="text-[10px] font-semibold uppercase opacity-30">Pulse</span>
      <Skeleton animation="pulse" height={36} />
    </div>
    <div className="flex flex-col gap-1">
      <span className="text-[10px] font-semibold uppercase opacity-30">None</span>
      <Skeleton animation="none" height={36} />
    </div>
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>
