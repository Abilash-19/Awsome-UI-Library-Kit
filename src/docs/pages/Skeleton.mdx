Skeletons are used to indicate that a piece of content is loading. They provide a better user experience than blank spaces or spinners by maintaining the layout's structural integrity.

## Basic Usage
Skeletons can mimic any shape from text lines to profile pictures.

<ComponentPreview code={`<Skeleton variant="circular" width={64} height={64} />
<Skeleton variant="text" width="80%" />
<Skeleton variant="rectangular" height={100} />`}>
  <div className="w-full max-w-sm p-6 border rounded-[2rem] space-y-6" style={{ borderColor: 'rgba(255,255,255,0.05)' }}>
    <div className="flex items-center gap-4">
      <Skeleton variant="circular" width={56} height={56} />
      <div className="space-y-2 flex-1">
        <Skeleton variant="text" width="60%" height={16} />
        <Skeleton variant="text" width="40%" height={12} />
      </div>
    </div>
    <Skeleton variant="rectangular" height={160} className="rounded-2xl" />
    <div className="space-y-2">
      <Skeleton variant="text" height={12} />
      <Skeleton variant="text" height={12} />
      <Skeleton variant="text" width="70%" height={12} />
    </div>
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>

## Customization

Choose between a smooth pulse or a dynamic wave effect.

    </div>
  </div>

### Shape Variants
| Variant | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| `text` | Simulates a line of text with specific scale and radius. |
| `circular` | Perfect for avatars or round icon placeholders. |
| `rectangular` | Standard box shape for images or large blocks. |
| `rounded` | Rectangular with a larger 12px border radius. |
