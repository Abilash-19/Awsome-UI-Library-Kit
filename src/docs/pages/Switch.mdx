## Usage

Use for immediate actions, like system preferences or binary settings. Avoid for settings requiring a "Save" action—use Checkboxes instead.
Ensure labels are clear and phrased positively (e.g. "Enable Notifications").

<ComponentPreview code={`<Switch label="Email Notifications" description="Receive daily summaries." />`}>
  <div className="flex flex-col gap-4">
    <Switch label="Email Notifications" description="Receive daily summaries." />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>

## Sizes & States

You can provide physical sizes, disabled states, and contextual errors or loading skeletons.

<ComponentPreview code={`<Switch size="small" label="Small Switch" />
<Switch label="Settings" error="Network error, can't save." />
<Switch disabled label="Unavailable" />`}>
  <div className="flex flex-wrap gap-6 items-start">
    <Switch size="small" label="Small Switch" />
    <Switch label="Settings" error="Network error, can't save." />
    <Switch disabled label="Unavailable" checked={true} />
  </div>
</ComponentPreview>
